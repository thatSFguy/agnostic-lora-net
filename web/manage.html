Battery (fw ≥ 0.6.0)

reading: —
Measure the battery with a meter, enter the value (volts like 4.02, or mV like 4020), then Calibrate. The node derives its ADC scale from that one reading, stores it in flash (survives firmware updates), and reports battery as mV + % from then on.
